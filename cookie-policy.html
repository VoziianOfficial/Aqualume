OVERVIEW

Cookies and browser storage can help keep your experience consistent.

This Cookie Policy describes how Aqualume may use cookies, local storage, and similar technologies when you browse the website, manage privacy settings, or use platform features.

Aqualume is an independent provider-matching platform. Cookie or browser storage choices are used to support website functionality and preference controls; they do not change your ability to make your own decisions about provider options.

You can manage optional categories at any time through the Cookie Settings control in the site footer, cookie banner, or this page.

02

COOKIES AND LOCAL STORAGE

These tools may store limited information in your browser.

Cookies are small text files that websites may place in a browser. Local storage is a browser feature that can retain certain website information on your device. Similar tools may help a website remember settings, support security, or understand how features are used.

On Aqualume, browser storage may be used to remember the cookie preferences you choose. This helps the platform avoid repeatedly showing the same consent choices during future visits from the same browser.

Cookies

Small browser files that may support website sessions, functionality, or enabled optional tools.

Local storage

Browser-based storage that can remember settings such as your cookie preference choices.

Optional categories may not always be active on the platform. Their availability can depend on the website tools enabled at a given time and the preferences you select.

04

HOW PREFERENCES WORK

Your selection is saved locally in your browser.

When you make a choice through the Aqualume cookie banner or Cookie Settings panel, the website stores that selection in your browser. This lets the website recognize your preference during future visits from the same browser.

  • Essential functionality remains active because it supports core website operation and privacy preference storage.
  • You may accept all categories, reject optional categories, or choose individual optional preferences where available.
  • Updating your settings changes the preference stored in the browser you are currently using.
  • Clearing browser cookies or local storage may remove previously saved preferences and cause the consent banner to appear again.
05

THIRD-PARTY TOOLS

Some enabled website tools may be provided by third parties.

Aqualume may use third-party services to support website hosting, performance, analytics, communication, security, or other platform functions. When those tools are enabled, they may use their own cookies or similar technologies in accordance with their applicable policies.

Aqualume does not control third-party browser tools, policies, or practices. Review the relevant third-party privacy materials when they are made available or when you use a third-party service directly.

Optional third-party tools should only be enabled when they are appropriate for the platform and consistent with the preferences you have selected.
