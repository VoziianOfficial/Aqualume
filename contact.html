BEFORE YOU START

A few simple details can help create a clearer request.

You do not need photos, measurements, or a technical diagnosis to get started. The most helpful information is simply what you are noticing in the home right now.

Where is the concern?

Mention the room, level, or nearby area where water, dampness, or visible changes are appearing.

What are you seeing?

Note visible water, damp surfaces, staining, material changes, or anything else that stands out.

When did it begin?

Share whether the situation is active now, recent, recurring, or something you have noticed over time.
